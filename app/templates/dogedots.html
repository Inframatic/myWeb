Doge Dots

Design, XCode, Responsive, ASO, Animation, App Publishing

Play Doge Dots (pronounced 'dohj') on the IOS App Store and link your way to victory! Connect vertically or horizontally for combo points & coins. Connect your line back to the original dot your finger started at to form at least a square, and reach the highest possible score like the bamf you are! Focus your mind, and chain together as many Doge as you can. The power is yours! For ages 4 years and older Doge Dots Gameplay
